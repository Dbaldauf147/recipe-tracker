Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

Prep Day ("we", "our", "the app") is a meal planning application. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you create an account or sign in using Google, Facebook, or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from the provider. We also store the recipes, meal plans, and preferences you create within the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To sync your recipes and meal plans across devices
  • To enable social features like sharing recipes with friends

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore). Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication — for sign-in (Google, Facebook, Apple, email/password)
  • Cloud Firestore — for storing your recipes and app data
  • Vercel — for hosting the application

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. See our Data Deletion page for instructions.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via the app's GitHub repository.
